The website for the Scotland Office has been replaced

On 30th January 2013 the website for the Scotland Office was replaced by gov.uk/decc.

GOV.UK does not cover every piece of content that used to be found on the website for the Scotland Office. The page you are looking for is probably one of these.

Essential government services and information can be found at GOV.UK.

A copy of the page you were looking for can be found in The UK Government Web Archive, however it will not be updated after 30th January 2013.
